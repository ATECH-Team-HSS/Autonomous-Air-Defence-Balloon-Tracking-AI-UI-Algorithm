# ATECH Team Members and Contributions

This project was developed by the **ATECH Team** as an educational robotics and computer-vision prototype. The system combines AI-based balloon detection, real-time tracking, a PyQt5 control-station interface, PCB/control electronics, and CAD/CAM mechanical design.

## Project Contribution Areas

The ATECH Team contributed across the following engineering areas:

* **AI and Computer Vision:** YOLO-based balloon detection, real-time frame processing, target classification, and tracking support.
* **User Interface:** PyQt5 control-station interface, mission controls, system logs, status indicators, and operator workflow.
* **Control Electronics:** PCB design, MCU communication, control-system logic, and hardware integration.
* **Mechanical Design:** CAD/CAM design of the turret mechanism and control-station structure.
* **System Integration:** Combining AI, UI, hardware control, and mechanical subsystems into one educational robotics prototype.

## Contribution Statement

Each member contributed to a specific subsystem of the project. The final prototype represents the combined work of the AI vision, UI, control electronics, PCB, and mechanical design teams.

This repository documents the software and system-level integration work for the **Autonomous Air Defence Balloon Tracking Prototype**, using balloons as safe educational targets for demonstrating AI-based tracking and control-station operation.
